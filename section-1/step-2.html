Step 2: Learn About The Types Of Protests

Once you know exactly what you will be protesting and why. You can then begin determining how you want to protest. Since this guide is a 101 guide, we will mostly cover attending and participating in protests, not how to organize them.

Typically, when talking about protests, people think about demonstrations, marches, and rallies and they group these three under the umbrella of a protest. But there are many other types of protests, there is no one way to attend a protest. A protest itself is an action, not an event, but events can be categorized as protests.

Click Here to learn more about the basic types of protests and see which types you think might want/be able to attend. It is important to know your limits, as some types of protests are more strenuous than others. Know that there are other types of protests, but they might not be organized as commonly.
