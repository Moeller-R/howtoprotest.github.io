Protest Dos And Don'ts

Do: Stay updated on your rights as protester and citizen Follow permit terms as required Dress for the weather Bring food, water, and other supplies Know your limits physically, mentally, and morally Don’t: Block walkways, entrances, pedestrians, or traffic unless allowed to by a permit Bring any weapons or things you don’t want to be seen with you by authorities Enter onto private property without permission Interact with counter protesters Threaten anyone or make calls to violence Destroy or vandalize any property
